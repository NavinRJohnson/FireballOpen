Course Information

Pebble Beach Golf Links

Par: 72

Yardage: 7,075 yards

Rating/Slope: 74.7/143

Signature Hole: 7th - 106 yard par 3 with dramatic ocean views

Notes: Consistently ranked as the #1 public course in America. The course hugs the rugged coastline, providing wide-open vistas and dramatic shots over cliffs and coves.

Spyglass Hill Golf Course

Par: 72

Yardage: 6,960 yards

Rating/Slope: 75.5/147

Signature Hole: 4th - 370 yard par 4 with a green nestled in the dunes

Notes: One of the most difficult courses in the world. The first five holes wind through the coastal dunes, while the rest of the course features elevation changes through the Del Monte Forest.

The Links at Spanish Bay

Par: 72

Yardage: 6,821 yards

Rating/Slope: 73.8/145

Signature Hole: 13th - 176 yard par 3 with ocean backdrop

Notes: A true Scottish-style links course with rolling fairways and challenging bunkers. Don't miss the bagpiper who closes the course each evening.
