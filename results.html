Tournament Format

Our trip will feature a friendly competition with the following format:

Individual Competition

Format: Stableford Points

Handicaps: USGA handicaps will be used

Scoring:
• Double Bogey or worse: 0 points
• Bogey: 1 point
• Par: 2 points
• Birdie: 4 points
• Eagle: 6 points
• Albatross: 8 points

Prize: "Pebble Beach Champion" trophy and bragging rights

Team Competition

Format: Ryder Cup Style

Teams: Team A vs Team B (4 players each)

Matches:
• Day 1: Four-ball (best ball)
• Day 2: Foursomes (alternate shot)
• Day 3: Modified Scramble
• Day 4: Singles matches

Prize: Dinner paid by losing team on final night

Daily Contests

Closest to the Pin: Par 3s on each course

Longest Drive: Designated hole each day

Most Pars or Better: Daily winner

Fewest Putts: Daily winner

Prizes: Small daily prizes from the pro shop
