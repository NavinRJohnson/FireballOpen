Travel Tips

Packing for Golf Clubs

• Most airlines charge for golf bags as checked luggage

• Use a hard-sided travel case for protection

• Remove club heads from adjustable drivers if possible

• Add extra padding around club heads

• Consider shipping clubs ahead via Ship Sticks

Airline Policies

• Check in online 24 hours before departure

• Arrive at the airport at least 2 hours early

• TSA PreCheck recommended if available

• Download airline apps for updates

• Keep confirmation numbers handy
